ZaP Electronicz is a sibling-owned electronics repair and customization business founded by Zyra and Pearse. We specialize in controller repair, console restoration, electronics cleaning, custom modifications, and electronics mentoring.

Our Story

Zyra and Pearse started their electronics repair journey with a simple passion: bringing broken devices back to life. What began as a hobby quickly grew into a thriving business built on trust, expertise, and genuine care for every device that comes through our doors.

Our Passion

We're driven by gaming, technology, learning, and creativity. Every repair is an opportunity to solve a puzzle. Every modification is a chance to enhance what someone loves. Every mentoring session is a moment to inspire the next generation of tech enthusiasts.

Our Mission

We believe in giving electronics a second life. Whether it's saving a cherished console, fixing a favorite controller, or teaching someone the art of repair, we're committed to making technology accessible, repairable, and fun.

FAQ

How long do repairs take?

Most repairs take 2-5 business days depending on the complexity. We'll give you an accurate estimate when you contact us. Urgent repairs may be available with expedited fees.

Do you accept mail-in repairs?

Yes! We offer convenient mail-in repair services. Simply package your device securely, include a detailed description of the issue, and we'll handle the rest. You can track your repair throughout the process.

Do you repair all consoles?

We repair most modern and retro consoles including Nintendo, PlayStation, Xbox, and Sega systems. Some rare or damaged systems may be outside our scope. Contact us to discuss your specific device.

Can I learn from you?

Absolutely! We offer mentoring sessions for all skill levels. From electronics basics to advanced repair techniques, we can tailor a learning program that fits your goals and experience level.

How do I request a quote?

Simply use our contact form below or reach out via email, Instagram, or TikTok with details about your device and issue. We'll respond within 24 hours with a quote and timeline.

Do repairs include a warranty?

Yes! All repairs come with a 30-day warranty on parts and labor. Extended warranties are available for additional protection and peace of mind.
